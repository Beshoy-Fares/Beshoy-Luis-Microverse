Google Story and important years

  • 1995 - La historia de Google comienza en 1995 en la Universidad de Stanford.


  • 1998 - En agosto de 1998, el cofundador de Sun, Andy Bechtolsheim, les dio a Larry y Sergey un cheque por USD 100,000 and more over The “Google.com” domain name is registered, and a new search engine joins the internet.


  • 2000 - The search engine is made available in 10 new languages (German, Danish, Spanish, Finnish, French, Italian, Dutch, Norwegian, Portuguese and Swedish).

  • 2004 - Gmail is launched in its beta version, initially offering 1GB of web email storage.

  • 2005 - Google Maps debuts, with maps and itineraries for the US, Canada and the UK.

  • 2006 - Google purchases YouTube, the video sharing platform, for over $1.6 billion.

  • 2008 - Google presents Chrome, its first web browser.

  • 2009 - The Mountain View firm announces plans for the Google Chrome operating system, based on Linux.

  • 2011 - Google presents the first Chromebook, manufactured by Samsung and equipped with the Chrome OS.

  • 2012 - Sergey Brin wears the first Google Glass prototype, whetting tech fans’ appetites for a future augmented reality offering from the brand.
